The window that's open right now

TrueFoundry is already framing Portkey as "application-scoped, not organization-wide."

That characterization isn't accurate today — but it could become true in the market's perception if it goes unanswered. This is the real danger of staying in the engineering room too long.

You said your priority is governance for agentic AI. Nobody owns that category yet. The company that defines what "AI governance platform" means gets to decide whether competitors are features or alternatives.

The conviction that built something 24,000 organizations rely on doesn't need to change. The vocabulary does — because the CFO and the CISO need to hear "AI that never breaks" as the same promise the engineering team does.
