The connective thread

Friction.

The word that stays constant across all three rooms. When you are unsure which version of the story to tell, return to friction. It works everywhere.

One word, three meanings

Investors

Friction reduction = platform efficiency and unit economics

Quote speed: 1 day → 30 min. No truck rolls for quoting. Remote-first cost advantage.

Contractors

Friction reduction = overhead elimination and calendar management

No lead fees. Automated design and permitting. DOE/ACCA certified sizing.

Homeowners

Friction reduction = simplicity and trust

No in-home visit needed. Money-back guarantee. 20% average savings.

25 → 50 people

The mission needs to carry
without the missionary.

Right now the origin story comes through because you are in the room telling it. At 50 people across multiple states, you will not be in every room.

Write it down. Two paragraphs. Your father's business, the overhead killing contractors, the decision to leave aerospace. Put it in the onboarding deck. Not as a poster — as the answer to "why did we build it this way?"

Your edge

You grew up in the trades, studied at MIT, worked at NASA, and came back to build software for the people your father worked alongside.

That is a credibility bridge that connects every room in this framework. Nobody else in the home electrification market can tell that story.

The challenge is not whether the story is good. It is whether the story scales.
